About AudioScope

Intelligent Hi-Fi Analysis

A free AI-powered reference tool built for audiophiles and anyone making informed hi-fi purchasing decisions.

What Is AudioScope?

AudioScope is a free online comparison tool that lets you evaluate 2–6 hi-fi audio components side by side in seconds. Whether you are choosing between integrated amplifiers, phono preamplifiers, turntables, DACs, streamers, speakers, or headphones, AudioScope retrieves detailed technical specifications, pricing, dimensions, editorial summaries, and curated resource links — so you can make confident, well-informed decisions without hours of research.

How It Works

AudioScope is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. When you enter a component's brand and model name, our system queries the AI for a comprehensive technical profile. The AI draws from manufacturer specifications, published reviews, and established audio databases including Stereophile, What Hi-Fi, Audio Science Review, and The Absolute Sound.

Each comparison retrieves:

  • Full technical specifications with proper engineering units (dB, Hz, Ω, W, mm, kg)
  • Physical dimensions and weight in both metric and imperial
  • MSRP pricing and year of introduction
  • Notable product features and design highlights
  • An editorial summary of the component's sonic character and ideal use case
  • Key strengths and honest considerations
  • Links to manufacturer pages, professional reviews, and YouTube demos

Supported Categories

  • Integrated Amplifiers — Entry-level to reference-grade integrated amps
  • Preamplifiers — Standalone line stages and control preamplifiers
  • Phono Preamplifiers — MM and MC phono stages for analog playback
  • Turntables — Belt drive, direct drive, and idler wheel designs
  • Tonearms — Pivoted and tangential tracking arms
  • Phono Cartridges — Moving magnet, moving coil, and moving iron
  • DACs — Desktop, portable, and network-connected converters
  • Streamers — Network audio transports, streaming DACs, and Roon endpoints
  • Loudspeakers — Floorstanders, standmounts, and active speakers
  • Headphones — Over-ear, in-ear monitors, and planar magnetic designs

Tips for Best Results

  • Include both brand and full model number — "Rega Planar 3" not just "Rega"
  • Include the version for different generations — e.g. "Naim Nait XS 3"
  • For phono cartridges, include the stylus designation — e.g. "Ortofon 2M Blue"
  • Mixing price tiers in one comparison often reveals the best value options
  • All components in a session must be from the same category

Accuracy & Disclaimer

Specifications are AI-generated from manufacturer documentation and reputable published sources. While the system strives for accuracy, errors can occur — particularly for very recent releases or limited-edition variants. Always verify critical specifications with official manufacturer documentation before purchase.

AudioScope is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any audio equipment manufacturer.

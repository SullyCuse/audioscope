1. Introduction

Welcome to AudioScope ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at audioscope.netlify.app. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: AudioScope does not require account creation, registration, or login. The only information you submit is the audio component names you type into the comparison tool. These queries are transmitted to our secure serverless function solely to retrieve specification data and are not stored after the request is fulfilled.

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit AudioScope, certain information may be collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify) and third-party services, including: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and approximate geographic location derived from IP address. This information is used for analytics, performance monitoring, and site security.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality. Cannot be disabled.

  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These may be based on your visit history on this and other websites. See Section 4 below for details and opt-out options.
